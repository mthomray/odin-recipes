Cheeseburger

Description

The recipe for a classic cheeseburger requires a good few items, but the end result is certainly delicious. There's a reason it's a staple of American culture.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patties
  • Cheese slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Take a pan and butter it
  2. As the butter melts, throw the patties on
  3. While the patties cook, start spreading mayonnaise on the buns
  4. Add ketchup, mustard, onions, tomatoes, lettuce, and pickles
  5. Place the cooked patty on top of the bottom bun, and place sliced cheese on top of the patty
  6. Put the top bun on, and enjoy
