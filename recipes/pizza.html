Pizza

Description

The recipe for a classic pepperoni pizza is fairly simple. The end result is going to be a salty, savory, delicious pie, that people have enjoyed for centuries.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll the dough into a flat circle shape
  2. Take a tablespoon and spread the tomato sauce over top of the dough
  3. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top
  4. Apply as much pepperoni as you like
